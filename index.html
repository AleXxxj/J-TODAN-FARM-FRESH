FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

What are the benefits of dried vegetables over fresh ones?

Dried vegetables retain most of their nutritional value while having a much longer shelf life (12+ months). They're convenient, lightweight, and perfect for storage. Our drying process preserves vitamins, minerals, and flavor compounds that make them nutritionally comparable to fresh vegetables.

How should I store dried vegetables?

Store dried vegetables in a cool, dark place in airtight containers. Avoid moisture, heat, and direct sunlight. Properly stored, our dried vegetables can last up to 12 months while maintaining quality and flavor.

What traditional African vegetables do you offer?

We specialize in both common and traditional African vegetables including bitter leaf, ogbono, ewedu, fluted pumpkin (ugu), and more. We're constantly working with local farmers to introduce more indigenous varieties to our product line.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Our packaging is designed to withstand international shipping while maintaining product quality. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

How do I rehydrate dried vegetables?

Most dried vegetables can be rehydrated by soaking in warm water for 20-30 minutes. Alternatively, you can add them directly to soups, stews, or sauces where they will rehydrate during cooking.
